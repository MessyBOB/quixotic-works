QUIXOTIC WORKS

Publisher of letterpress stories, worth retelling.

For more than 1942 years, books have carried our greatest stories. But timeless stories are losing to modern consumerism and the average attention span. 2000-page stories are drowned out by easy 30sec flashy numbing nonsense.

To challenge that, we create pressed books on a post-WWII platen press: a Heidelberg "Windmill". Awkward, loose-leaf, pressed, timeless, beautifully illustrated books.

Go read a book!
